## Description (max 16,000 chars)

Find and fix accessibility issues on any website without leaving your browser. AllyProof runs the industry-standard axe-core engine locally on the page you're viewing and reports every WCAG 2.1 / 2.2 AA violation it finds — with severity, the failing element, the WCAG success criterion, and an "open in dashboard" jump to the AI-suggested fix.

WHO IT'S FOR
Web agencies and freelancers maintaining client sites under ADA Title II, the EU Accessibility Act, or Section 508; in-house developers shipping accessibility fixes; and accessibility consultants doing site-by-site audits.

WHAT THE EXTENSION DOES
• One-click WCAG 2.2 AA scan of the current page
• Severity-coded badge on the toolbar so you can see issue counts at a glance
• Side panel with the full violation list — dock right, left, bottom, or detach as a floating window
• "Show on page" buttons highlight the failing element in the live DOM
• Local-first: results live in browser storage; nothing is sent to AllyProof unless you sign in

WHAT YOU GET WHEN YOU CONNECT AN ALLYPROOF ACCOUNT
• Save scans to your dashboard for trend tracking and team sharing
• AI-rewritten fix code on every violation (powered by Claude)
• Multi-page crawl — kick off a full-site scan from the toolbar and watch results land in the dashboard
• DRAFT VPAT export for procurement responses
• Weekly auto-scans across every site in your workspace

PRIVACY-FIRST DESIGN
• No <all_urls> host permission — the scanner only runs on the tab you ask it to
• No analytics, no tracking pixels, no remote code loading
• chrome.storage.sync is never used — your tokens never leave the device they were minted on
• Open privacy policy at https://allyproof.com/privacy

OPEN STANDARDS
Powered by axe-core (Deque Systems, MIT). Score and severity classifications match the AllyProof dashboard so the number you see in the popup is the same number your team sees in the report.

PRICING
The extension is free. The local quick-scan, side panel, badge, and "Show on page" highlights work without any account. AllyProof account features (cloud save, AI fixes, crawl, VPAT, weekly scans) start at $79/month — see https://allyproof.com for plans.
